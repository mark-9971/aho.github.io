Our Apple Varieties

Honeycrisp

Crisp, sweet, and juicy. A fan favorite for eating fresh and making delicious pies.

Ripens: Mid-September

Granny Smith

Tart and excellent for baking. Holds its shape well and provides a wonderful tangy flavor.

Ripens: Early October

Fuji

Sweet and firm, great for eating fresh. Known for its long storage life.

Ripens: Late October
